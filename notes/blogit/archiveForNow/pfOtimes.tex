In Zee's Quantum Field theory book he writes

\begin{align*}
\gamma^0 &= 
\begin{bmatrix}
I & 0 \\
0 & -I
\end{bmatrix}
=
I \otimes \tau_3 \\
\gamma^i &= 
\begin{bmatrix}
0 & \sigma^i \\
\sigma^i & 0
\end{bmatrix}
=
\sigma^i \otimes \tau_2 \\
\gamma^5 &=
\begin{bmatrix}
0 & I \\
I & 0
\end{bmatrix}
=
I \otimes \tau_1 
\end{align*}

The Pauli matrices $\sigma^i$ I have seen.  However, I have two questions

1) What are these $\tau_i$ matrices?
2) I am not familiar with this $\otimes$ notation.
