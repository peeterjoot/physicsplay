\section{Curl of curl}

\index{curl}
\index{vector identities}
Expanding the \(c\) component of \(\spacegrad \cross (\spacegrad \cross \BA)\) we have

\begin{dmath}\label{eqn:crossCrossA:10}
{\left(\spacegrad \cross (\spacegrad \cross \BA)\right)}_c
=
\partial_{a}
{\left( \spacegrad \cross \BA \right)}_b \epsilon_{a b c}
=
\partial_{a}
\left( \partial_{m} A_n
\epsilon_{m n b} \right)
\epsilon_{a b c}
=
\partial_{a}
\partial_{m} A_n
\delta_{c a}^{[m n]}
=
\partial_{a}
\left(
\partial_{c} A_a
-\partial_{a} A_c
\right)
=
\partial_c (\spacegrad \cdot \BA)
- \spacegrad^2 A_c
\end{dmath}

or
\begin{dmath}\label{eqn:crossCrossA:30}
\spacegrad \cross (\spacegrad \cross \BA)
=
\spacegrad (\spacegrad \cdot \BA)
- \spacegrad^2 \BA
\end{dmath}
